Consider IVF Process to Avoid Bad Things

We know that having a baby with IVF or In Vitro Fertilization will cost expensive. That is why some people think twice to do this process. As it is said in two previous parts, we need to consider success rate of IVF and cost of IVF before we do IVF program. When you have already considered two things then you need to know about IVF process. Some people think that it is a simple process. Unfortunately, it is a complicated process for women.

Most women call this process as roller coaster process. Women will wait for certain time to know whether the process of IVF program is a success or not. Most women and men finally choose to do IVF program because they want to be free from infertility. As we know infertility will be able to increase stress level in women and men. Women and men will feel so sad when they see some other couples have a baby.

Reason of Choosing IVF Program

There are some couples that finally choose IVF program because this program is the effective method to get a baby. Women have freedom to use their own eggs or get eggs from a donor. Most people choose to do this program because there are some people who have already got a baby after they do this program. You can get examples from some celebrities too. They have already got baby in fast time after they do this program. When we think about the cost of IVF program, we still can save our money to get better result of IVF program.

Consultation Step

The first step that you must do when you want to do IVF process is consultation. It is so important to do the consultation with your doctor and you must bring your partner. Doctor will check all things and your partner body too. Doctor needs to see women’s fallopian tubes too and then check men’s sperm to know whether sperm and eggs are healthy or not. It is the first step that must be done before you do serious step. The next serious step is doing X-ray process or ultrasound process. Doctor will check all things deeper by using this technique.

Get Stimulation Hormones

After you do a consultation, your doctor will give you prescribe drugs and also stimulation hormones. You need to consume pills or get an injection. You must consume drug and get the injection for two weeks. You need to consume drug at the beginning of your menstrual cycle. When you use your own egg, the doctor will put your egg and then fertilized your egg in a tube. When your eggs are fertilized, your eggs will be transferred back to your uterus with procedures.

When you want to get your baby with this program, you must do IVF process from consultation to fertilization. You must visit your doctor regularly to know your condition and development of your baby. The cost of IVF program must be prepared. You need to choose good clinic to do your program. After you consider about the success rate of IVF program, the cost of IVF program and the process of IVF program, you will get your baby in a good way.
