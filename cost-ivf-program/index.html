Type of IVF Cycle

Your health or your body condition will influence the price of IVF program. There are some types of IVF cycles such as using your own eggs, using your own eggs with a surrogate, using donor eggs, and also using donor eggs with a surrogate. When you don’t use your own eggs then you will pay a higher price. You must find best donor egg or sperm for your IVF program. You should be able to choose a high quality woman or men who want to give healthy egg or sperm to you. When you get egg or sperm from donor you must pay donor fees, lab and also screen fees and some other things.

The other factor that will influence the cost of IVF program is your age.
